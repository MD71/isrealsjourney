Isreals Journey

Our little Israel is the most thoughtful, kind, wonderful, and LIVELY four year old that has such a passion for life. He was diagnosed with A.L.L. Leukemia on June 26, 2018. In the past month, he as had multiple Chemotherapy treatments, two bone marrow biopsies, (one being a bone chip), three spinal tap procedures, and 30 days of induction chemotherapy.

The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP) has been amazing to Israel and the Soucier Family. Israel will have to fight this battle for the next 3.5 YEARS which will be extremely challenging for Israel and his family. Israel has 3 brothers; Cole 14 yoa, Isaiah 10yoa, and Eden 2 yoa. The Soucier Family and the community have come together with so much unconditional LOVE and SUPPORT during this most difficult time.

Between the costs of medical bills, lost wages, and cost of travel to Philadelphia from the Jersey Shore several times weekly and a multitude of associated expenses is overwhelming. We are asking for support and prayers from our friends, family, and community to assist with the stress that this unexpected medical and financial burden has brought on the loving Soucier Family.
